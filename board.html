Puan Sri Datin Kamu Palan

Puan Sri Kamu Palan started her career as a teacher in 1986 with the Ministry of Education and later as a corporate trainer with SMR Group before joining the board of SMRT Holdings Berhad for a few years.

She pursued a NCC Diploma in Computers (1987) from the Systematic Computer Centre, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia; a Certificate in Training & Development (1992) from the Institute of Training & Development, U.K.; and a Diploma in Management (2003) from the Institute of Administrative Management, U.K.
