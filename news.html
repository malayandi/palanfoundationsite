Applications Are Now Open for 2018!

Applications are now open for all candidates interested in applying for the Palan Fellowship. The Palan Fellowship is a unique fellowship programme run by the Palan Foundation together with the Cyberjaya University College of Medical Sciences (CUCMS). The Fellowship serves to support talented, disadvantaged undergraduates at CUCMS both financially and professionally. To learn more about the Palan Fellowships, click here.

In special cases, the Foundation may offer financial support to outstanding Malaysian students not enrolled at our partner institutions who are financially disadvantaged. If you believe yourself to be such a student and would like to apply for support from the Foundation, please send us a email by following the link on our contact page.
